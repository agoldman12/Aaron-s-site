SMCKC

I have been a part of the Social Media Club of Kansas City for a little over a year. I found out about the club while attending another event called Kansas City Geek Night. One of the people I met at Geek Night was the events chair for SMCKC and she told me I should come to their happy hour.

After going to the SMCKC happy hour I was hooked. I met so many cool people I decided to go to their friday breakfast. The breakfast is once a month on friday morning. There is always a speaker and about 60-70 people ususally attend.
