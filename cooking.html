Cooking

How did I get into cooking, you may ask. Well, I will tell you.....

It starts way back when I was little. You see, I have always been a creative person. I love to sing, I did drawing and painting when as a kid, and I played guitar. So it is only natural that eventually I found my way into the kitchen.

The main catalyst for going into the kitchen was moving away to college. There are only so many frozen dinners and dining out that one can handle. So I decided needed to learn how to cook.

JCCC Hospitality Management Program

I was working at Marisco's Pasta and Seafood restaurant in Lawrence, KS when I learned about the hospitality managment program at Johnson County Community College. I wanted to learn how to cook, so I decided to sign up.

I started as a chef apprentice but I couldn't find a chef to work under so I switched to food and beverage management. I learned how to cook some in class, but I didn't really learn how to cook until I worked with Sam Cross and Zach Churchill at the Peppercorn Duck Club. They were the two best chefs I have every worked with and they really made me want to be a great chef.
